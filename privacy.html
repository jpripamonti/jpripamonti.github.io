Privacy

This website does not enable analytics by default and does not track email clicks, outbound links, file downloads, or scroll depth.

The site loads fonts from Google Fonts and links to external services such as GitHub, Dataverse, ORCID, Google Scholar, LinkedIn, and publishers. Those services may receive standard browser request information when you open their links.

If analytics is enabled in the future, this page should be updated before deployment to describe the provider, collected events, retention settings, and opt-out or consent behavior.

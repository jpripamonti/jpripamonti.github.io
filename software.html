suacir

An R package that downloads citizen request data (SUACI) from the Buenos Aires City Government's open data portal and automatically cleans and repairs inconsistent latitude and longitude coordinates.

DualAudio

A macOS application currently in early development that plays audio through two output devices at once, allowing two people to listen to the same music or watch a movie together. It supports setups such as two Bluetooth headphones or a combination of wired and Bluetooth devices.

Download pkg

Ludus Scaccorum

A single- and two-player chess analysis game based on real gameplay. It presents positions drawn from a selected player's recent games, and players evaluate them by choosing the best move. Scoring depends on how closely their choices align with the engine's recommendation.

Repository Play Game
